Daily planning & time management

This article was last updated: 5 hours ago

尽量写完！

Background

The concept of time is important

有时候我意识到人这一生很短暂，有很多事情我们都来不及做。虽然每个人拥有的时间是一样，毕竟每个人都以小小小于光速运动着。那么真正能改变一个人的时间就是做事情的效率以及合理的安排。 简单来说我们管理时间的目的就是为了提高时间的利用效率 它能够延长我们生命的长度。

我本并不是一个高度自律的人， 但我想通过这样的方式去改变我自己。

Self-reflection

一下是我个人总结的一些小经验

Daily planning

The morning

The most energetic and concentrated time of the day

04:00 | Wake up |

God Morning!

04:00-04:30 | Morning exercise |

—Wake up the body—

When you wake up in the morning, your body is usually still stiff, and the most natural wake-up mechanism is “stretching.” But what we have to take is to further awaken the whole body, such as: esay exercise, meditation.

04:30-05:15 | Writing blog, planning and reflection |

The unexamined life is not worth living. -Sokrates

Indeed, understanding reflection can make oneself stronger. At the beginning of a new day, our mentality will be more peaceful, and we will be more rational when thinking and reflecting on things. Therefore, there will be a fairer and calmer attitude to reflect on what happened yesterday. This makes it easier for us to find what needs to be corrected and improved.

—Start the brain—

Simple planning:
1.Write a morning diary. It mainly records what happened the previous day and is worth recording, as well as reflections.

  1. Write a plan for the day. Organize the to do lists, and fill blockytime
  2. Write blog.

05:15-05:45 | Out and walk |

Just a short walk to freshen up. I guess you will be tired, so go out and get some fresh air! (optional)

06:00-07:30 | Study 1.5h |
Now is the time to study!

7:30-7:45 | Breakfast |

Preferably a porridge with protein drink, eg milk or soy drink. As well as a fruit !!

8:00-12:00 | Study 4h |

Time to study

The day

高效

12:00-13:00 | Foodtime and lunchbreak |

I have no choice with that time for lunch. If it is a regular lecture day, I am free from 12:00 to 13:00, which is then suitable for lunch and lunch puash.

13:00-13:30 | Take a naps |

I have tried other times to take a nap in the afternoon. I think the optimal time is between 13: 00-13: 30. If it’s later than that, I’ll get really tired, then I will not keep it in time. Sometimes it happens that I take a too long nap maybe 1h30min. As a result, I can not sleep at night.

Regardless, I think taking a short nap is a good recovery for the day. Especially for me who wake up early in the day. If in that case you miss that time, I recommend not to do it on another occasion. You can instead have a cup of coffee or similar.

13:30-17:15 | 4h. |

study

17:15-18:00 | Sports / Reading / Violin |

I would like to have an aerobic workout every other day. In the best case, it is every day. The sport I like most is cycling and jogging. Jog at least 5km. So far, I have no good optimal for how much I have to cycle.

If not, I want to have that time to read or practice the violin!

18:00-19:00 | Other |

Here you have the time for a few other things. Very important and make good use of it !!

The evening

The time is controllable, the mood is relaxed and happy, and does not affect early going to bed.

19:00-19:30 | Foodtime and short break |

Time for supper. I usually only cook in the evening. So the time will vary a little now and then. On average, I need 1 hour and a little more to cook.

19:30-21:30 | Free times |

DO whatever you want.

21:30 | Prepare to bedtime |

For the last half hour, I want it to the shower and toothbrushing.

As well as absolutely the most important, mediation or any way you can relax, or reduce stress. !! Very important !!

Another thing I experienced where it can interfere with my sleep was the telephone. Avoid the phone 30 minutes before going to bed. It’s important !!

22:00 | Bedtime and goodnight |

Turn off the lights and sleep. Going to bed early is the basis for you to get up early the next day. Sleeping at a fixed time every day helps to adjust the biological clock, to ensure that you have a better state to face the challenges of the next day, so that your mind is calm, creative and efficient.

Sum Up

  • Minimum 6 hours sleep and optimal 7.5h
  • Preferably a hot drink before I go to bed
  • Try to meditate at least once a day
  • Exercise at least 4 times a week
  • Study 8h
  • Max 2 cup coffee per day
